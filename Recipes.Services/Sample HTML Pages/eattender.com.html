Ingredients

Sous Vide Potatoes Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs small red skinned potatoes
  • 2-3 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • Canola oil

Aioli Ingredients

  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • Fresh lemon juice - 1/2 lemon
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Set your sous vide water bath to 85ºC.
  2. Seal your potatoes, olive oil, and salt in either a vacuum seal bag or a zip seal bag using the water displacement method. Make sure they they are not overlapping. The potatoes might float, so make sure they're fully submerged. Sous vide for one hour.
  3. While your potatoes are in the water bath, make your aioli.
  4. In a blender or food processor, blend egg yolk, garlic, 1/4 tsp. salt, and 2 tsp. water. Slowly drizzle in extra virgin olive oil, in a slow steady stream, until sauce is thickened and emulsified. Stir in cayenne and season aioli with lemon juice, pepper, and salt. Refrigerate until potatoes are finished.
  5. After an hour, remove potatoes from water bath and let dry on a cookie rack. In a cast iron skillet, fill with 1 inch of canola oil and turn on high heat.
  6. Gently smash the dried potatoes so that they're a little flattened. Gently drop the potatoes into the oil, making sure they don't overlap. Fry until the skin is golden brown and crispy.
  7. Once golden brown, remove from skillet and dab excess oil with paper towel. Serve in a bowl with a generous drizzle of aioli and some herbs.
